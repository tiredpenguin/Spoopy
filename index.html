Ghosts and Keys

  • High Concept: Collect keys and escape as the ghost becomes faster
  • Player Experience Goals: Spookiness
  • Genre: Arcade/Horror
  • Game play: A player must traverse their way through darkness to find the key while running away from the ghost who constantly follows you. The ghost becomes faster you score higher.
  • Prototype goal: Using darkness and randomizing elements
  • Features: It's increasing difficulty every level and randomized items
  • Setting/Premise/Story: The guy is stuck in a perpetual hell that is this game. He must escape from the ghost.
  • Target Audience: Regular audience
  • Play time: Less than 1 minute
  • Strategies: Remembering where the obstacles are and treading carefully wins the game
  • Prototype Assets: I made the assets from scratch except for the sounds
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This is similar to other horror games out there
